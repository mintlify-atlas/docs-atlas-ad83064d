Welcome to the **AI System Prompts Collection** — the most comprehensive and actively maintained repository of system prompts, instructions, and model configurations from leading AI tools and platforms.

## What is This Collection?

This collection provides unprecedented insight into how the world's leading AI tools, coding assistants, and intelligent agents are built. With over **30,000+ lines** of carefully extracted and documented prompts, this repository serves as an invaluable resource for:

- **Developers** building AI-powered applications
- **Researchers** studying prompt engineering and AI behavior
- **Product teams** designing intelligent user experiences
- **Security professionals** understanding AI system architectures
- **AI enthusiasts** learning from production implementations

The collection includes system prompts and configurations from:

- **Cursor** - Agent, CLI, and chat prompts

- **Replit Agent** - AI pair programming prompts
- **Augment Code** - Claude and GPT agent prompts

## Why This Matters

<Note>
Understanding how leading AI systems are prompted gives you insight into:
- Best practices for prompt engineering
- System architecture patterns
- Safety and security considerations
- User experience design for AI interfaces
</Note>

These prompts represent thousands of hours of engineering effort and iteration by some of the best AI teams in the world. By studying them, you can accelerate your own AI development and avoid common pitfalls.

## Open Source & Community-Driven

This collection is released under the **GNU General Public License v3.0**, ensuring it remains free and accessible to the community. The repository is actively maintained with regular updates as new tools emerge and existing ones evolve.

<Warning>
**For AI Startups**: If you're building AI products, ensure your system prompts and configurations are secure. Exposed prompts can become targets for exploitation. Consider using security services like [ZeroLeaks](https://zeroleaks.ai/) to audit your AI systems.
</Warning>
